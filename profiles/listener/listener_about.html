About Cosmic Harmony

What is Cosmic Harmony?

Cosmic Harmony is a web-based radio station that delivers the hottest beats across multiple genres.

We aim to connect music lovers, DJs, and producers to unify the music.

1 universe. 1 harmony.

Where are we located?

We are located in Northern Virginia! We started as a humble student idea in the George Mason University as a MUS101 project. There, we worked with SWE432 students to design the website and pioneer a new website!

Our headquarters are in a rich part of the United States, filled with many diverse cultures and people. We are music-lovers influenced by the hottest trends and the past classics, the Western hits and the International bangers. You listen to it, well so do we!

We embody the expression in our music, and Northern Virginia is the hub to do so. Of course, we have visitors from all over the world!

The Cosmic Harmony Vision

When we look at the stars, we see how the universe aligns across light years to produce a lyrical consonnance.

We aim to bring producers and DJs together to bring listeners a wide variety of music genres.

After all, music cannot be divided into one shape, one note, one chord. Music is diverse, flowing, growing.

We are the first station to combine multiple genres in one station.
